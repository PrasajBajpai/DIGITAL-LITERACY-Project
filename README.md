2.Cybercrime Case Study (UPI Fraud)

Overview:
UPI (Unified Payments Interface) fraud is a common cybercrime where users are tricked into authorizing payments.

How it happens:

Fraudster contacts victim (call/SMS/WhatsApp)
Builds trust or creates urgency
Sends collect request or QR code
Victim enters UPI PIN (thinking money will be received)
Money gets transferred to fraudster

Target Audience:

Students
Elderly people
Small business owners

Consequences:
Instant financial loss
Emotional stress
Difficulty in recovering money

3. Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
Use strong passwords and enable 2FA
Never share OTPs or UPI PIN
Avoid suspicious links and unknown apps
Don’t use public Wi-Fi for banking
Log out from shared devices
Keep software updated

UPI Safety Tips:

Never approve unknown collect requests
Don’t scan QR codes to receive money

Report Cybercrime:
https://cybercrime.gov.in
 Helpline: 1930
